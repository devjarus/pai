# Evidence Pack

## Problem

Describe the user-visible or metric-visible problem and the owning architecture block.

## Change

Summarize the code and doc changes that were made.

## Validation

Record the commands run, with pass/fail status and the highest-signal result.

## Outcome

Record the behavior change or metric movement that shows the change helped.

## Risks

Record residual risks, tradeoffs, or open questions that remain after the change.
