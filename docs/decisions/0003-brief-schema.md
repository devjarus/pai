# 0003 Brief Schema

## Decision

Standardize Briefs around a recommendation-first schema.

## Why

Without a stable brief shape, the product drifts toward raw research dumps, implementation detail leakage, and inconsistent trust signaling.

## Tradeoff

- the schema constrains output flexibility
- the constraint improves readability, comparability, and validation

## Implications

Every meaningful Brief should make room for:

- recommendation
- what changed
- evidence
- memory assumptions
- next actions
- correction hooks
