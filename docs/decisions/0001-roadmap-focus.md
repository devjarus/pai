# 0001 Roadmap Focus

## Decision

Focus `pai` on the recurring decision loop instead of positioning it as a broad personal AI platform.

## Why

The repository already has strong memory, background execution, and report-generation capabilities, but the product identity becomes weak when every feature family is treated as equally important.

## Tradeoff

- lose short-term breadth and some demo surface area
- gain a clearer product wedge and tighter prioritization

## Implications

- Programs and Briefs become the product center
- breadth is frozen unless it strengthens the core loop
- internal nouns should not lead the product story
